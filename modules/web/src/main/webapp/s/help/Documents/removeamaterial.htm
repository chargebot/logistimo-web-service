Remove a material

To remove a material, perform the following steps:

1.  Go to Setup > Materials.

2.  Check the box adjacent to the user's row.

•      You can also search for a user by entering his first name (including partial names) in the Search field and click Search button.

3.  Click Remove Materials button.

4.  A pop-up box will appear for confirmation.

5.  Click OK to remove material(s) (or Cancel to exit).
