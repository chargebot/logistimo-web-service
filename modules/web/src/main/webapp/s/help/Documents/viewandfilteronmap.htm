View and filter on map

To view and filter on map, perform the following steps:

1.  Go to Inventory > Stock views

2.  At the top right of the page, click on the Map icon

3.  A map view appears, with entities marked in the map.

•      The entities may be clustered on the map for better visibility.

•      So you can click and drill down to any of the clusters.

•      Or you can check ‘Do not cluster points’ on the bottom of the map to get an uncluttered view of the map.

4.  Click on any one of the map pins, to view the stock at that location.

5.  You can browse entity tags at the top of the map, if available to filter the map view by tags.

6.  To view stock by material on the map, start typing the material name in the ‘view by material’ search field at the bottom right of the map.

7.  The map pins are colored in yellow, orange or red as per the stock status.
