Export Reports

To export transactions, perform the following steps:

1.  Go to Reports

2.  Select the type of report you wish to export in the Type search field.

3.  On the top right of the page, click ‘Show export options’ link.

4.  Click the From and To time fields.

•      Select the date range from the drop down calendar.

5.  A pop-up box will appear for confirmation.

6.  This will export reports you select as a monthly report in form of spreadsheet (CSV file) to your e-mail inbox.
