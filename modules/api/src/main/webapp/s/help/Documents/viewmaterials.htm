View materials

1.  Go to Setup > Materials.

2.  Lists of materials in the domain are shown.

3.  Click on any material you wish to view

•      You can also search for a material by entering the material name (including partial names) in the Search field at the top left of the user domain and click search.

4.  View the materials details in the list

•      The name of the material, description, tags, the retailer’s price of the material, batch and last updated view.

•      If material tags are shown, you can also browse by material tags.
