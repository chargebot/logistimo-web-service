Edit a material

To edit a material, perform the following steps:

1.  Go to Setup > Materials.

2.  Click on the material name you wish to edit.

•      You can also search for a material by entering the name (including partial names) in the Search field and click Search button.

3.  At the bottom of the material profile page, Click Edit material button.

4.  Edit the desired fields of the material information.

5.  Click Save to retain the edited information of the Material (or Cancel to discard the edited details).
